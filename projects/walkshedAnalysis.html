--- title: Walkshed Analysis and Summary subtitle: Using Tableau To Support Transit Planning Decision Making Process layout: "page" icon: order: hide: true ---

When determining where to locate future bus stations, key criteria transit agencies consider are the number of people and jobs within a 5 or 10 minute walk of the proposed station. By comparing the 5 and 10-minute walksheds certain bus stations can be prioritized if they have high access while other stations may be passed over. To more easily draw these comparisons, I created a Tableau Dashboard that displays the total population, BIPOC population, and total number of jobs within the walkshed, when the user hovers over (or selects) a given station. The bar chart on the far right allows users to select a variable of their choosing to view the walkshed value for all stations along the corridor from north to south to allow for easy corridor-wide comparisons.

(Elements on the dashboards can appear compressed on smaller screens. For optimal viewing please view on a tablet or computer.)
